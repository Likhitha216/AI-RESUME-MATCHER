# AI-RESUME-MATCHER

🚀 CareerIQ – AI Resume Matcher

Bridge the gap between your resume and your dream job.
CareerIQ is an AI-inspired Resume Matcher that analyzes resumes against job descriptions, calculates compatibility scores, identifies strengths, highlights skill gaps, and provides actionable insights to improve job application success. ✨

📌 Overview

In today's competitive job market, tailoring a resume for every application is essential. CareerIQ helps job seekers understand how well their resume aligns with a specific job role by performing keyword matching and generating personalized feedback.

The platform provides a clean and intuitive interface where users can upload or paste their resume, enter a job description, and instantly receive a detailed analysis.
